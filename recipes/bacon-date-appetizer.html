Bacon Wrapped Date Appetizer

An easy, quick appetizer that I first tasted at a Native American wedding ceremony.
Dates are stuffed with almonds and wrapped in bacon!

Details:

Prep: 30 mins
Cook: 5 mins
Total: 35 mins
Servings: 6

Equipment:

  • A pack of toothpicks (packsize varies on desired serving size)
  • 1 baking sheet
  • Aluminum Foil (optional)

Ingredients:

  • 1 (8 ounce) package of pitted dates.
  • 4 ounces of almonds.
  • 1 pound sliced bacon.

Directions:

  1. Preheat the broiler.
  2. Split dates.
    Place one almond inside each date.
    Wrap dates with bacon, using toothpicks to hold them together.
  3. Broil for 10 minutes, or until bacon is evenly brown and crisp.
